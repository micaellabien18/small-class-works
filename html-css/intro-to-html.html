Weather

Cloudy
- entire sky is covered by clouds.
Light Wind
- wind having a speed of 4 to 7 miles (about 6 to 11 kilometers) per hour.
Drought
- a long period with no rain or unusually low levels of rain or other precipitation.
Luminous Clouds
- are tenuous cloud-like phenomena in the upper atmosphere of Earth.
Cold
- having a relatively low temperature.
Humid
- the presence of water vapor in the atmosphere.
Duststorm
- strong winds bearing clouds of dust.
Hot
- a period of unusually high temperatures.
Rainy
- large amounts of rain that fall at a particular time of year.
Foggy
- an obscuring haze, as of atmospheric dust or smoke.
